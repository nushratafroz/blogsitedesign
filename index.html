Welcome to NR's TRAVEL BLOG

In this world almost everything is uncertain. What we really can rely on is our experiences. When I first travelled, it seemed like a whole new world has been opened to me. The culture, the people, their daily lives, the food, the nature, their works, the homes, small roads; everything seems new, everything is interesting.And I realized I just can't stay at one place. There is so much to see, so much to experience, so much to live for. So here I am.
Underrated is the word that fascinates me. Everybig cities are being toured by hundreds of people every year. But I generally find beauty in the smallest of things. So my first goal is to travel some most underrated cities in the world.I had started with the small cities of my own country. But now I am off to Europe. Hop in anytime you want!
